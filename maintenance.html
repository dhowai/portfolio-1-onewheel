step 1

First use the thin brush to sweep off any debris from the board

step 2

Use the thick brush to loosen the dirt from the grip tape

step 3

Spray the surface cleaner on the underside of the board

step 4

Wipe down the board using the papper towels

step 5

Use the abrasice cleaning stick to loosen more of the dirt from the grip tape
