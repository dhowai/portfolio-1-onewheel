How to maintain a onewheel

List of items

Maintaining the board is cruial for the longevity of the boards life and having a good experience. This ofcourse depends on what kind of terrain you prefer to ride on. Here is a list of supplies you can use to maintain your board.

  1. Thin Cleaning Brush
  2. Thick Cleaning Brush
  3. Surface Spray
  4. Paper Towels
  5. Abrasive Cleaning Stick

Let's Get Cleaning

step 1

First use the thin brush to sweep off any debris from the board

step 2

Use the thick brush to loosen the dirt from the grip tape

step 3

Spray the surface cleaner on the underside of the board

step 4

Wipe down the board using the papper towels

step 5

Use the abrasice cleaning stick to loosen more of the dirt from the grip tape
